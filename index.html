How it works

STEP 1

Say what you need

Type it or speak it, in your own words — "need someone to fix my fan today" works just fine.

STEP 2

Get matched nearby

See workers ranked by how well they fit, how close they are, and how their past customers rated them.

STEP 3

Book and pay safely

Chat, agree on the work, and pay through the app. Your payment is only released once the job is done.

STEP 1

Set up your board

Add your skills, your prices, and photos of your work. Takes a couple of minutes, no online experience needed.

STEP 2

Get found nearby

Customers looking for exactly what you do, close to where you work, can find and book you directly.

STEP 3

Get paid on time

No chasing payment after the job. It's held safely and released to you the moment the work is confirmed done.

Skill isn't the problem. Being found is.

Most skilled workers we've talked to don't struggle with the work itself — they struggle with being seen. No contacts to ask around, no online presence, no easy way for someone three streets over to know they exist. Kaarigar exists to close that gap: putting real skill in front of the people who need it, without either side needing to already know someone.

It understands how you actually talk.

Search in plain Hindi, English, or a mix of both. Say what you need out loud if typing's not convenient. Kaarigar reads the intent behind the words — not just keywords — and ranks results by real fit: the right skill, close by, with a track record you can trust.
